title: "Andy Fang"
---

Andy Fang is an engineering manager at [Citadel](https://www.citadel.com) in New York, where he leads a team building firm-wide risk analytics infrastructure. He previously worked on cloud infrastructure and distributed systems at [Airbnb](https://www.airbnb.com/), and studied Computer Science at [Georgia Tech](https://www.gatech.edu/), where he published research in data visualization and HCI with the [Polo Club of Data Science](https://poloclub.github.io/).

Andy is also an instrument-rated private pilot. He flies out of [Essex County Airport](https://airnav.com/airport/KCDW) and is a member of [Paramus Flying Club](https://flyingclub.org).
